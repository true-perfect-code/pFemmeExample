Chart display
This modal helps you understand trend and tendency charts that are calculated based on your last recorded cycles.
Bleeding tendency
This graph shows the intensity and change in volume of your bleeding over time. It helps you visually see if your periods are getting lighter, heavier, or are stable and consistent from cycle to cycle.
Pain trend
It tracks the level and frequency of pain (cramps and other symptoms) you've recorded. With this graph, you can monitor whether the pain is decreasing or increasing, which is extremely useful for talking to the doctor.
Cycle length deviation
The bars on the chart show the exact number of days each of your individual cycles lasts.
Green line:
